Thank you for using **Legal Section-Student**.

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This page explains how users can delete their account and associated data.

## How to Delete Your Account

Users can delete their account directly from within the Legal Section-Student mobile application by following these steps:

1. Log in to your Legal Section-Student account.
2. From the bottom navigation bar, navigate to the last icon named **“My Account”**.
3. On the My Account page, tap the **Edit** icon located next to your name.
4. You will be redirected to the **Account Settings** page.
5. Tap the **Delete Account** button.
6. Confirm the deletion request.

Once confirmed, your account will be permanently deleted.

## What Data Is Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

* Personal information (such as name, email address, and phone number)
* Account credentials
* Any data associated with your account usage

This action is irreversible.

## Data Retention

Account deletion is processed immediately or within a short processing period.

No personal data will be retained after successful deletion unless required by applicable law.

## Important Notes

* You must be logged in to delete your account.
* If you are unable to access your account, you may request account deletion by contacting us.
